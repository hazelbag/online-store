Click and Collect Orders

If ordered by no later than 15h00 you can collect the item the same day. Payments for click and collect will be processed in store only and no online payments allowed.


Standard Delivery

Standard delivery times vary depeniding on location, outlaying areas can be from 3 - 5 days and major centres from 2 - 3 days. Payments are to reflect and if paying into our bank account proof of paymemt is to be emailed to us.


Next Day Delivery

If you place an order, selected next day delivery and pay using our secure payment channel before 12h00 (12pm) your order will be dispatched for delivery the next day. This option is only available from Monday to Thursday.


Weekend Delivery

Weekend deliveries are availble. Orders needs to be placed the before 10h00 for weekend deliveries. Your item will be delivered over the weekend between 13h00 and 14h00. This option is not available to outlaying areas.
